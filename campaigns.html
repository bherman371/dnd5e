Lost Mine of Phandelver

The Lost Mine of Phandelver is contained in the Starter Set.

Summary: A dwarf named Gundren Rockseeker meets you in a tavern in the city of Neverwinter and asks if you can bring a wagon of provisions to the town of Phandalin. You will be paid 10 gold pieces each for escorting the wagon safely to Phandalin.

Dragon of Leilon

I created Dragon of Leilon.

Summary: The characters are catching up in a tavern in the city of Starmetal when a messenger from the King enters the tavern out of breath. He hurries over to them, having recognized them from their previous adventure to the Wave Echo Cave. He has a mission from the King: You will be paid 100 gold pieces upon the rescue of my knight from the dragon near the town of Leilon. The messenger informs them that they will be met by four knights in the Stonehill Inn upon arriving at the town. The messenger thanks them for their time and bids them luck.
