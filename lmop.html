Part 1: Goblin Arrows

Description: The adventure begins as the player characters are escorting a wagon of provisions from Neverwinter to Phandalin. They travel south along the High Road and then veer east on the Triboar Trail. They run into some trouble with some goblins.

Part 2: Phandalin

Description: The characters arrive in Phandalin to find clues as to where Wave Echo Cave is located. They talk to various NPC's and take up side quests.

Part 3: The Spider's Web

Description: The characters wander out beyond Phandalin to follow up on the clues they found in Phandalin.

Part 4: Wave Echo Cave

Description: The characters find Wave Echo Cave. They begin their final battle against the Black Spider.
